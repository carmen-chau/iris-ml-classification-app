About the Iris Flower

Background information about the flower

Iris is a genus consisting of over 300 flowering plant species ("Iris"). The flowers are six-petaled and are symmetrical in shape.

This application deals with 3 of those Iris species: Iris-setosa, Iris-virginica and Iris-versicolor.

The petals of an Iris flower can be separated into 2 different types: Sepals and petals. Sepals are differentiated from petals. Sepals are defined as modified leaves encasing the flower, and in the case of the Iris flower, are longer than petals ("Sepal - Flower Part").
